ZujuGP's architecture is to allow immediacy of creation, sharing of and collaboration on content, experience and playing.

You may be looking for opportunities to upskill, gain employment or watch a game, shop, play and interact. This will no longer be something that only one person does. You won't need a new standalone app or travel to a stadium or find new ways to access different components of what interests you. What you want will be found on a single platform.

This platform is ZujuGP.

Where immediacy of use and interactivity of content will directly contribute to your enjoyment, allow you to connect globally and profit in your love for the sport.

The future of football will be mostly social, collaborative and participative, defined by immersion, UGC, dynamic and malleable experiences online.

It will not always be authoritarian or top down. All football fans and professionals will be both users and creators. Football on ZujuGP is also an outlet for those who seek creative and commercial interests.

The collective experience that allows ZujuGP to be better is that football will no longer be a walled garden. ZujuGP will be an online immersive that mirrors our world.
